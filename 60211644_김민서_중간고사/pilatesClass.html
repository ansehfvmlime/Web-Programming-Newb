Class Descriptions

Intro Reformer Pilates: An introductory class focusing on the core principles of Pilates and familiarization with the Reformer apparatus. Suitable for beginners with no prior experience.
Mat Pilates A floor-based class emphasizing core strength, flexibility, and body awareness using minimal equipment.
Reformer Flow A dynamic session utilizing the Reformer to enhance strength, coordination, and balance through continuous movement sequences.
Cardio Sculpt Combines Pilates principles with cardiovascular exercises to elevate heart rate while toning the entire body.
Pilates Stretch & Recovery Focuses on gentle stretching and mobility exercises to aid recovery and improve flexibility.
1:1 Private Session: Personalized Pilates sessions tailored to individual goals and needs.
